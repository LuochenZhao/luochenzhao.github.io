Short biography

I did my undergraduate in Peking University, and was gratefully supervised by Prof. Ruochuan Liu. I was in the PhD program of Rutgers-New Brunswick for two years before I got transferred to Johns Hopkins and finished my PhD; my academic advisor was Antonio Lei and my department advisor was Yiannis Sakellaridis.
